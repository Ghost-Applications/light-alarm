Privacy Policy

Light Alarm does not collect any personally identifiable information from its users.

The only data collected by the app are crash reports, which are used solely for the purpose of improving the application's stability and performance. Crash data is collected and processed by Firebase Crashlytics, a service provided by Google. This information helps us identify and fix issues to provide a better user experience.

No other data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties. By using Light Alarm, you agree to the collection of crash information as described above.
